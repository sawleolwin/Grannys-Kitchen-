About Us

Our Story

We believe that homemade food has a special place in everyone's heart. Our specifically crafted menu aims to arouse your imagination to return to your childhood, your native town, rich culture, family affection, and memories of Burma. We are delighted to welcome you to join us on this journey.

Founder

Saw Leo Lwin

With a profound passion for Burmese cuisine, a relentless pursuit of perfection, and a desire to represent the homemade food of his home country on a global scale, owner and founder, Leo has always sought to educate himself with numerous renowned restaurants around the world. As a result, he has gained valuable knowledge and competence, allowing him to embark on his first venture into the culinary sector.

Leo is quickly radiating the influence and experience of his undying love for Burmese food into the Hong Kong community, with the desire to fill a specific need in the nostalgic local dining scene. Come by to meet the family that brings you directly to your childhood table.
